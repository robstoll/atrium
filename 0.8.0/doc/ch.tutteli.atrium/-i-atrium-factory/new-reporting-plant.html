Creates a ReportingAssertionPlant which checks and reports added Assertions.

It creates a newThrowingAssertionChecker based on the given reporter for assertion checking.

Parameters

subject - The subject for which this plant will create/check Assertions. (see BaseAssertionPlant.subject).

reporter - The reporter which will be used for a newThrowingAssertionChecker.

The newly created assertion plant.
